Admissions

Use this page to clearly explain how parents can enroll their child.

How to apply

  1. Visit the school office or contact us
  2. Collect/submit the application form
  3. Submit required documents
  4. Confirm admission & fee details

Documents (example)

  • Birth certificate / Aadhaar
  • Previous report card (if applicable)
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photos
